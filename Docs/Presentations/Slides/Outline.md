To satisfy the requirements of the course, your presentation should:

1. Explain your design problem and your solution to it;
2. Show the audience that your team has done design work at a high level. Specifically, show
- that you used design methodology – not ad-hoc decision-making,
- that you used engineering analysis to support design decisions
- that you have concrete, quantitative evidence demonstrating that your design meets or exceeds requirements
3.Encourage audience members to visit your poster;
4. Use good presentation skills to make preceding points informative, persuasive and enjoyable.
